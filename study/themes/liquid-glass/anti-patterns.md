# Anti-patterns

- Flat opaque backgrounds (defeats the glass illusion)
- Sharp corners without border-radius (glass wants rounded edges, 12-16px)
- Heavy drop shadows (glass is light — use subtle, diffuse shadows)
- Dark thick borders (glass borders are luminous, not structural)
- Text on blur without contrast insurance (illegible)
- Neon or saturated accent colors (glass wants muted, translucent tones)
- Dense operator-style layouts (glass needs breathing room)
- Busy textures behind the glass (makes blur noisy instead of smooth)
