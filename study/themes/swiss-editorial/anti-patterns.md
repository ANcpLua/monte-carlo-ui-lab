# Anti-patterns

- Gradients of any kind
- Drop shadows or box shadows
- Rounded corners > 4px (Swiss is sharp or barely rounded)
- Colored backgrounds for cards (use white space and grid alignment instead)
- Icon-heavy interfaces (text labels are clearer and more Swiss)
- Decorative borders or dividers (use space)
- More than one accent color
- Emoji in UI
- Animated decorations (Swiss is static by nature)
- Busy textures or patterns
